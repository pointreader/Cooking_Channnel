Momo: Momo is very popular dish in Nepal

Ingredients:
  • Keema
  • Cilantro
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Masala
Cooking Process:
  • Prepare Keema and other items
  • Mix all ingredients
  • Wrap it
  • Put some butter
  • Steam it in the steamer for 15 mins
Enjoy momo with your favorite sauce!!!
